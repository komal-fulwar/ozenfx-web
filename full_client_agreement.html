Client Agreement

1. Introduction

This Client Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into by and between Ozenfx Markets Ltd (hereinafter referred to as the "Company") and the Client, which may be a legal entity or a natural person (or persons) who has completed the Application Form. The Agreement governs CFD trading activities between the Client and the Company.

2. Interpretation Of Terms

The following terms are defined for clarity in this Agreement:

  • Access Data: Refers to one or more user identification codes, authentication codes, and other information required to access the Client Terminal.
  • Account Type: Signifies the different types of accounts made available to the Client, including Classic, Copy Trading, and ECN/STP accounts.
  • Affiliate: Any entity directly or indirectly controlling, controlled by, or under common control with the Company.
  • Agreement: Includes this document and other incorporated policies like Withdrawal & Refund Policy, Risk Warning, and Privacy Notice.

3. Commencement And Account Activation

The Agreement becomes effective upon the opening and funding of the Client's Account and remains binding upon both parties.

4. Client Categorization

Clients are categorized as Retail, Professional, or Eligible Counterparties, affecting their regulatory protections. The categorization is based on the Client's Application Form and provided information.

5. Capacity

The Client acts as a principal and not as an agent, trustee, or custodian unless explicitly authorized by the Company in writing.

6. Third Party

Clients may authorize third parties to act on their behalf only with prior written consent from the Company.

7. Personal Data & Confidentiality

The Company is committed to maintaining the confidentiality of the Client's personal data and using it solely for the purposes outlined in this Agreement.

8. Services

The Company provides trading services, including order execution and other services, as detailed in this Agreement. No investment advice is provided.

9. CFDs’ General Trading Procedures And Orders

Details on order placement, execution, margin requirements, and other trading procedures are outlined in this section.

10. Margin Requirements

Clients must maintain the required margin levels as specified by the Company. The Company has the right to adjust margin requirements without prior notice.

11. Swap

Islamic (swap-free) accounts are available to comply with Shariah law. Clients must follow Company guidelines for account conversions.

12. Confirmations

Trade confirmations and account status updates are provided via the Company's Online Trading System.

13. Decline Of Client’s Orders, Requests, And Instructions

The Company reserves the right to decline any orders, requests, or instructions that do not comply with the Agreement or applicable laws.

14. Miscellaneous

Additional terms regarding account suspension, reactivation, and other provisions are detailed in this section.

17. Termination

Either party may terminate the Agreement with written notice. Termination does not affect pre-existing obligations.

18. Severability

If any provision of the Agreement is deemed unenforceable, the remaining provisions will remain valid and enforceable.

19. Force Majeure

20. Client Acknowledgements Of Risk And Consent

Clients acknowledge the risks associated with CFD trading and agree to the terms of this Agreement.

21. Amendment
