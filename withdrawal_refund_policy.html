2. Submission Process

Withdrawal requests should be made through our secure online portal (available on our website ozenfx.com) or via email from the registered email address. Send withdrawal requests to support@ozenfx.com.

5. Currency of Withdrawal

Withdrawals are processed in the currency of the trading account. Conversion fees may apply if funds are withdrawn in a different currency.

6. Mode of Withdrawal

Withdrawals can only be processed using the same method as the initial deposit. Exceptions may be made in special cases, such as a change in citizenship or account closure, subject to verification and approval.

7. Third-Party Withdrawals

Withdrawals can only be made to accounts held in the name of the Ozenfx account holder. Third-party withdrawals are not permitted.

8. Cancellation of Withdrawal

If you wish to cancel a withdrawal request, contact our customer support team immediately. Cancellation is subject to approval, and fees may apply.

9. Limits

Ozenfx may impose minimum and maximum withdrawal limits. These limits vary based on account type and regulatory requirements. Details are available on our website or upon request.
